Aurora Supercomputer




Aurora has been the second fastest supercomputer in the world since
2023. Aurora was sponsored by the United States Department of Energy
(DOE) and designed by Intel and Cray for the Argonne National Laboratory.

Functions include research on nuclear fusion, low carbon technologies,
subatomic particles, cancer and cosmology. It will also develop new
materials that will be useful for batteries and more efficient solar cells.
